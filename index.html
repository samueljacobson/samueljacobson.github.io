About

A native of Cleveland, I have recently returned to my hometown after 7 years in Chicago, and am eager to begin a career in software development.

While in Chicago, I completed a master's degree in the humanities at the University of Chicago, focusing in music history and theory. I then worked as as freelance music critic, covering Chicago's dynamic classical music scene, in tandem with a day job in human resources. In the case of the latter, I worked at companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500, and in so doing developed a strong business accumen.

Having always maintained a strong interest in science and technology, upon relocating to Cleveland I enrolled in We Can Code IT, an intensive full-stack development bootcamp. I am looking forward to leveraging my diverse set of prior experiences to offer a unique perspective in the field of software development.

Outside of development, my interests include classical music, traveling, and the New York Times crossword. Samples of my coding work follow; samples of my concert reviews can be viewed here and here.
